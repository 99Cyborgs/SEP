# Negative Results Policy

Negative results are part of the research program, not cleanup debris.

## Rules

1. Triggered failures are serialized and archived under `failures/`.
2. The negative registry remains append-only for substantive failures.
3. A theorem note or paper outline cannot silently ignore a benchmark family that fails its intended gate.
4. Surrogate benchmarks, such as the current delay-coupled pair, must carry their limitations into both positive and negative reporting.

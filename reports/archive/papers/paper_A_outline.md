# Paper A. Linear Spectral Theorem And Benchmark Confirmation

## Claim
In controlled autonomous linear systems with isolated slow carriers and weak reduced coupling, finite-horizon subsystem leakage obeys an affine leading-order law.

## Minimal Theorem Content
- T1 theorem in the normal or mildly nonnormal regime.
- Markov-chain analogue stated with the same leakage observable.

## Figure List
- spectrum and gap ladders
- projector deformation versus perturbation-to-gap ratio
- leakage traces
- predicted versus observed autonomy horizon

## Anticipated Criticism
This can be dismissed as standard perturbation theory with repackaged terminology.

## Response Strategy
Emphasize the subsystem leakage observable, the autonomy-horizon diagnostic, the failure taxonomy, and the preregistered benchmark/gate workflow.
